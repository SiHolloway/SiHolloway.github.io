About Si Holloway Consulting

Get to know Si Holloway

I have worked in talent and people development for over 20 years - the last 12 years I’ve specialized in development coaching, leadership development and talent development programs.

As an experienced and certified coach I’ve worked with people across all levels; from executives through to individual contributors. I became certified as a coach in the UK in 2011 through ILM (Institute of Leadership and Management). Since completing this extensive and accredited program, I have gained over 3000 coaching hours working with hundreds of coachees in organizations within Canada, the US, the UK and Europe.

I was born in London, England - but grew up and lived on the outskirts of the city of Brighton on the south coast of England. In 2014 I moved to Edmonton Canada with my wife after I accepted an opportunity to come and work with Ledcor a large North American construction company. Initially employed as an internal development consultant and coach, I worked with senior leaders and executives in one of their largest divisions. In just 3 years I had worked closely with over 50 leaders, providing an in house resource for coaching, development and succession planning, as well as becoming a thought partner and trusted advisor.

Prior to this I worked with BUPA (one of the largest global private medical insurance company - based out of the UK), and before that with British Telecom (the UK’s largest telecom service and network provider). I’ve held a number of different Talent, Leadership and Organizational Development roles at these organizations. It was at BUPA that I discovered my passion for coaching and was able to gain experience coaching individuals across all levels and at various points in their careers.

You can find more details of my work history by viewing my LinkedIn profile
